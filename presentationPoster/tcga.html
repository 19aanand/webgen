The Cancer Genome Atlas (TCGA)

What

A program begun in 2006 by the National Cancer Institute and the National Human Genome Research Institute

Mission

Characterize the genomic profiles of variouscancers through sequencing and bioinformatics

How

Genome Characterization Centers (GCC) performed the sequencing, Genome Data Analysis Centers (GDAC) integrated and analyzed data (Fig. 1)

Where

Tumor data/metadata is stored in the publically-available Genomic Data Commons repository (NCI, 2019)
