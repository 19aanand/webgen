A Vertically-Integrated Project

WebGen is a multidisciplinary team project in the Vertically Integrated Project (VIP) Program at Stony Brook University.

VIP teams bring together undergraduate students, graduate students, and faculty members to work on long-term projects in research, design, and innovation.
